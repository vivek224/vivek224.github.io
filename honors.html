  2. Invitee to the 2015 Heidelberg Laureate Forum: A forum providing an opportunity for graduate students and postdocs in Mathematics and Computer Science to interact with people who have shaped these two fields. Invitations to the forum are based on an application process.
  3. Invitee to the 2017 Early Career Program at the ACM/IEEE Supercomputing 2017 Conference: A workshop for researchers and professionals that completed their PhD recently and that are working in the field of high-performance computing, a subfield of Computer Science, to learn how to plan for a successful career in the area of high-performance computing.
